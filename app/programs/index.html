Programs

We offer a unique program that bridges the gap between research and practice. We teach theory, technology, and design cores in all our programs.

Part time or full time

We love our working students! They bring depth to classroom discussions with real work experiences. We’ll help you figure out the details with your company and schedule.

On campus or online

Master’s students can study online to help accommodate full or part time jobs. Our online students benefit from our close-knit community, even while abroad.

Our online graduate program is ranked third in the nation by the 2019 U.S. News and World Report.

ITLS Degree Comparison

Doctor of Philosophy

We offer four years of guaranteed funding to highly qualified PhD students. Ask a faculty advisor about our Doctoral Scholars Program.

Masters Progams:

Education Specialist - The Education Specialist (EdS) program furnishes students with advanced skills in instructional technology and learning sciences beyond those acquired with the master's degree.

Master of Arts - The goal of the Master of Arts program is to prepare individuals for careers in instructional design and development.

Master of Education - The Master of Education program is a degree program specifically designed for classroom teachers, educators, school librarians and administrators.

Master of Science - Students in the Master of Science program do research on a topic of their choice and write a thesis.
