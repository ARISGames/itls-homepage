ITSA Council

The Instructional Technology Student Association is free and all are welcome to participate! All ITLS students are automatically members. ITSA's mission is to create positive atmosphere wherein professional excellence and moral principles coexist. ITSA creates a great opportunity to network with fellow students, faculty, alumni, and potential employers.
