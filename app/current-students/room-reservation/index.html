Computer Labs

Overview

The computer lab is used as a classroom. When class is not scheduled, the lab is open to ITLS registered students. *All computers run Mac OS.

Door Access Codes

  • ITLS graduate students: contact the ITLS office (room 215) or your faculty advisor.
  • Students enrolled in an ITLS course: contact your instructor.

Guidelines & Security

  • Do not give out door codes or prop doors open.
  • The ITLS labs are not part of USU's open access computer lab system.
  • Do not bring food or drink into labs.
  • Save backup copies of your work. Hard drives may be reformatted at any time, without notice.
  • Use headphones for audio.
  • Shut down your computer after use.
  • Be considerate of others.

Equipment Checkout

Equipment is available at the ITLS main office (EDUC 215). ITLS enrolled students can check out video cameras, digital cameras, computers, headphones (mini-jack connector or USB connector,) 1 apple iPad, Video Recorder tripods, 3D printer, and more.

Guidelines

  • You must be enrolled in an ITLS course.
  • Equipment must be used on work for an ITLS course.
  • You are responsible to pay for damage or loss.
  • SD cards must be returned with the camera.
  • 2 day checkout period (depending on current demand.)
  • Return to ITLS main office (EDUC 215).

Check Out

  • Valid USU ID or Driver's License
  • No Reservations (first-come, first-served.)
  • ITLS Main office (EDUC 215)
